Enrollment at San Francisco High School

Enrollment Process

Thank you for considering San Francisco High School for your education. We welcome students from all backgrounds and strive to create a diverse and inclusive community.

To enroll, please follow the steps outlined below:

  1. Fill out the enrollment application form.
  2. Submit required documents, including transcripts and letters of recommendation.
  3. Attend an admissions interview, if necessary.
  4. Receive acceptance notification.
  5. Complete enrollment paperwork.
