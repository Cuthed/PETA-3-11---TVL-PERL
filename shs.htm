Senior High School at San Francisco High School

Senior High School Program

Our senior high school program provides students with a comprehensive and challenging curriculum to prepare them for college and beyond.

Key Features:

  1. Rigorous academic courses in various subjects
  2. Advanced Placement (AP) and International Baccalaureate (IB) programs
  3. Elective courses to explore students' interests
  4. College preparation and counseling
  5. Extracurricular activities and leadership opportunities

Strands in senior high:

  1. Accountancy, Business and Management (ABM).
  2. Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM).
  3. Humanities and Social Science (HUMSS).
  4. General Academic Strand. (GAS)
  5. TVL Track: Home Economics (HE).
